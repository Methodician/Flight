ABOUT

Jacob Johnston

A lover of science and technology, soaking up all the coolest new web development tools and methodologies. Jacob takes great pleasure in creating powerful, dynamic web applications and beautiful, intuitive interfaces.

He's done years of data science for big names like Intel and Nike. He learned a lot about displaying, interacting with, and analyzing data in intuitive, user-friendly ways that even an executive can indulge in. Over time he realized that what he loved most was creating things that people use and making the information and resources on the web more fun and enlightening.

So, he got into web application development, making tools for big and small companies along with philanthropic non-profits. The tools help them better manage their resources, save time and money, and make lives easier and more fulfilling.

Then he had another revelation: what's important is making peoples' lives better with the skills and knowledge he has gained over the years. It's fun and he wants to do it for you.

All he wants right now is to help make your business better with tools and applications.

Tricia Mendoza

Born with a forest green crayon in one hand and a keyboard and mouse in the other, from day one, Tricia was ready to tackle the technological trends that would affect the design world.

Making sure not to get tempted with the financial stability that comes with pursuing a career in the medical field, she stayed on track by achieving a Bachelors Degree in Illustration from California State University, Long Beach. She plans to fulfill her childhood prophecy of furthering her claim in the design community by using shapes, lines, and composition in ways they thought they never could be used before. Watch out for her, as she is just getting warmed up.

Tricia is a Freelance Interaction Designer who currently resides in the green-filled wonderland that is Portland, OR with her patiently supportive and encouraging significant other, Jacob, and their confidently introverted hamster Freddie Pepper who always graciously declines out-of-the-cage-time when the clock hits 7pm. When she is not busy fulfilling her prophecy, she enjoys hiking, drooling over the dribbble feed, and slaving away in the kitchen.
